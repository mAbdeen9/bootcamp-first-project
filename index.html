About The Carnival

Our Flag

The colors traditionally associated with Mardi Gras in New Orleans are green, gold, and purple. The colors were first specified in proclamations by the Rex organization during the lead-up to their inaugural parade in 1872.

Holiday names

In the United Kingdom, Most Tuesday. In German for the holiday several names, Schmutziger Donnerstag, Fetter Donnerstag, Unsinniger Donnerstag, Weiberfastnacht, Greesentag. Russian Масленица.

The holiday where and when

The holiday of Mardi Gras is celebrated in all of Louisiana, including the city of New Orleans. Celebrations are concentrated for about two weeks before and through Shrove Tuesday, the day before Ash Wednesday.

The rules of the masks

In New Orleans, costumes and masks are seldom publicly worn by non-Krewe members on the days before Fat Tuesday (other than at parties), but are frequently worn on Mardi Gras. Laws against concealing one's identity.
